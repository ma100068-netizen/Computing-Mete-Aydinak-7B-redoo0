Modern football has transformed from its ancient predecessor, the Chinese game of Cuju, evolving dramatically through several key stages into the global phenomenon it is today. Cuju, originally a military fitness exercise involving kicking a ball through a small goal, differed greatly from the highly structured, professional sport of modern football
